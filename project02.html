Music Visualizer

Languages & Software: Java; Eclipse, Processing

This program generates a visual image that reacts to different components of a song. Using the Mimim library, it analyzes different components of a user-chosen mp3 file and changes different components of the image depending on these components, such as song level.

The user has the option to pause and unpause the visualizer with the click of their mouse, and they also have the ability to change the colors by pressing a number that corresponds to a specific option from a curated selection of color schemes.

This is just one example of a music visualizer that I have made. After creating my first visualizer at a Java coding intensive a few summers ago, I have created a few iterations of music visualizers--they are one of my favorite side projects to toy around with!
